## 2. The Prompt (Copy & Paste)

> **Subject**: A professional startup logo for a B2B AI platform named "ProductLogik".
>
> **Visual Metaphor**: Integrate a "Brain" or "Chart" concept with a "Checkmark" or "Logic Gate". Think: Intelligence meets Decision.
>
> **Style**: Minimalist, Geometric, Tech-focused. Paul Rand style. Vector art. Flat design. No gradients, no shadows.
>
> **Color Palette**:
> *   Primary: Indigo Blue (#4F46E5)
> *   Background: Pure White
>
> **Typography**:
> *   Font: San-serif, bold, modern (like Inter or Helvetica).
> *   Text: "ProductLogik"
>
> **Layout**:
> *   Icon on the left, Text on the right.
> *   The icon should be simple enough to work as a Favicon (16x16px).
>
> **Negative Keywords**: --no complex details, --no photorealistic, --no shadows, --no 3d render, --no messy lines.
