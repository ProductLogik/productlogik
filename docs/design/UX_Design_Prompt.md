# AI UX/UI Design Prompt

**Tool**: Use this prompt in **v0.dev**, **Figma AI**, **Galileo AI**, or **Midjourney** to generate the initial design.

---

## The Prompt

**Role**: You are a world-class UI/UX designer specializing in B2B SaaS data dashboards. Your aesthetic is "Linear.app meets shadcn/ui" — clean, monochromatic, high-contrast, with subtle purposeful colors.

**Project**: "ProductLogik" — Logic-Driven Product Intelligence.
**Tagline**: An AI tool that tells Product Managers what to build next.

**Screen to Design**: The "Analysis Results Dashboard".

**Key Visual Elements**:
1.  **Sidebar (Left)**:
    *    Navigation items: "Uploads", "Analysis", "Settings".
    *    Bottom: User Profile & "Usage: 4/10 credits".
2.  **Header**:
    *    Breadcrumb: "Project: Q4 Customer Feedback / Analysis".
    *    Action Button: "Export PDF" (Secondary style).
3.  **Main Content Area (3-Column Grid)**:
    *   **Column 1: The Priorities (Executive Summary)**
        *   Three "Cards" stacked vertically.
        *   Card 1 (High Priority): "Fix Mobile Login". Red urgency tag. "34 mentions".
        *   Card 2 (Med Priority): "Export to CSV is slow". Orange tag. "12 mentions".
        *   Card 3 (Low Priority): "Dark Mode request". Blue tag. "8 mentions".
    *   **Column 2: Theme Explorer (List)**
        *   A vertical list of grouped feedback items.
        *   Each item is a collapsible accordion.
        *   Header of accordion: "Billing Issues (Confidence: 98%)".
        *   Inside accordion: 2-3 quotes similar to "I can't find the invoice."
    *   **Column 3: Agile Health (The "Anti-Pattern" Detector)**
        *   A distinct "Panel" or "Widget" looking slightly different (maybe dark background).
        *   Title: "Team Health Check".
        *   Content: "⚠️ Risk Detected: Feature Factory".
        *   Icon: Warning triangle.
        *   Text: "You shipped 40 points but user satisfaction dropped."

**Style Directives**:
*   **Font**: Inter or Geist Sans.
*   **Background**: #FFFFFF or very light gray #F9FAFB.
*   **Cards**: White background with subtle border #E5E7EB, no drop shadow.
*   **Accent Color**: Indigo-600 (primary actions).
*   **Error/Risk Color**: Rose-500 (specifically for the Agile Anti-Patterns).

**Generate a high-fidelity mockup showing this layout.**
